Ela Suites
at a Glance

Ela Suites is a boutique, 39-key premium suites property located in the heart of Hyderabad’s HITEC corridor. Ground + 4 upper floors, with a total super built-up area of ~17,470 sq.ft.

Each room is thoughtfully designed at approximately 155 sq.ft. — compact yet exceptionally comfortable, prioritizing smart layout and modern aesthetics.

Not a hostel. Not a five-star palace.
