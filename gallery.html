Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ela Suites?
A 39-key boutique hotel in Hyderabad’s HITEC area offering compact premium rooms.
Designed for business travellers, long-stay professionals, students, families and remote workers.
Where exactly is it located?
In the heart of HITEC catchment — minutes from HITEC City, HITEX, NIFT, IBS and only 2 km from Hitec City railway station.
Surrounded by offices, restaurants, malls and entertainment.
What kind of rooms do you have?
All rooms are efficient ~155 sq.ft, thoughtfully planned for sleep, work and storage.
Total 39 rooms across 5 floors, ~9 rooms per guest floor.
Who should stay at Ela Suites?
Business travellers, long-stay professionals, students/parents, families and remote workers.
Not a hostel, not 5-star — a modern, practical premium city base.
What facilities are available?
Elevator, 100% power backup, 480 sq.ft gym, 520 sq.ft all-day café + terrace, double-height lobby.
ADA-compliant, well-ventilated and safety-focused design.
